About Us

Meet the candidates of the Power Slate. We are committed to making a positive impact for the membership of Local 633.

John Smith

John Smith has been a dedicated member of Local 633 for over 15 years. With a background in logistics and a passion for workers' rights, John has been instrumental in negotiating fair contracts and improving working conditions for all members.

Emily Johnson

Emily Johnson is a seasoned union representative with over 20 years of experience. Her expertise in labor law and her commitment to advocacy have made her a powerful voice for the members of Local 633.

Michael Williams

Michael Williams brings a wealth of experience in labor relations and has been a strong advocate for safety in the workplace. His dedication to the well-being of union members is unwavering.

Sarah Brown

Sarah Brown has been a union steward for over 10 years, fighting tirelessly for equal rights and fair treatment. Her leadership and dedication have earned her the respect of her peers and colleagues.
